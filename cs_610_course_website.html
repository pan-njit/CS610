Course Description

This course focuses on algorithm design and analysis. The first half covers standard topics including sorting, dynamic programming, and classic algorithm paradigms. The second half explores advanced topics such as approximation and randomized algorithms for NP-hard problems like Vertex Cover and Coverage Maximization.

Prerequisites: CS506 or CS241, and CS505 or CS114. Students are expected to know discrete mathematics and probability theory.

Textbooks and References
